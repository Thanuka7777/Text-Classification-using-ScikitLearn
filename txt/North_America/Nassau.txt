Nassau is principally the name of the German town Nassau, which has given its name to many other places and entities, notably including the royal House of Nassau, the former independent state Nassau in modern Germany, Nassau County, New York, and the city of Nassau, Bahamas.Nassau may refer to:a noble dynasty originally from Germany and at present the monarchs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands:a noble dynasty originally from Germany and at present the monarchs of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg
